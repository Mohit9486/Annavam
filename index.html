Our Story

In a small village of Rajasthan, called Girawari, long before brands and barcodes, sweets were made with patience, intuition, and love.

Our grandparents began preparing Pedas in the 1970s ‚Äî not as a business, but as a way of life. Milk was slow-cooked, hands did the measuring, and taste was refined over decades, not days.

Over the years, something beautiful happened.

People carried our Pedas beyond the village ‚Äî to cities, to other states, to family gatherings and festivals. Every time, the feedback was the same: "This tastes different. This tastes real."

ANNAVAM was born from this journey.

ANNAVAM means lineage ‚Äî the passing down of knowledge, values, and taste from one generation to another. Every product we make carries that continuity: handmade, small-batch, honest, and uncompromising.
